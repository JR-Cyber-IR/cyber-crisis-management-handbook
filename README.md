# üö® Cyber Crisis Management Handbook

The **Cyber Crisis Management Handbook** provides a structured, practical guide to managing
**major cyber crises at the organizational level**.

This repository focuses on **decision-making, coordination, governance, and communication**
when a cyber incident escalates beyond technical incident response and impacts
business operations, reputation, legal obligations, or executive leadership.

---

## üéØ Purpose

This handbook helps organizations to:

- Understand what constitutes a **cyber crisis** (vs a technical incident)
- Structure and operate an effective **cyber crisis cell**
- Support **executive and board-level decision-making**
- Manage **internal and external communications** during a crisis
- Prepare, exercise, and improve cyber crisis readiness
- Capitalize on lessons learned after a crisis

It is designed to be **technology-agnostic**, **vendor-neutral**, and **international**.

## üß© How to Use This Repository

1. Start with **`fundamentals/`** to align on definitions and principles.  
2. Use **`governance/`** to define your crisis organization and authority model.  
3. Prepare your organization using **`preparation/`** before a crisis occurs.  
4. During a crisis, rely on **`crisis-response/`** and **`communication/`**.  
5. After the crisis, use **`post-crisis/`** to improve maturity and resilience.

This repository can be used as:
- A reference handbook
- A basis for internal crisis playbooks
- A support for tabletop and simulation exercises
