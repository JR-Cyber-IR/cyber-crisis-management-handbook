# Third-Party & Vendor Management

## Overview

This document addresses the management of third-party vendors, data processors, and supply chain partners during cybersecurity incidents.

## Vendor Notification Requirements

### Immediate Notification

1. **Data Processor Notification**
   - Notify data processors of incidents immediately
   - Provide details of data accessed/exfiltrated
   - Request confirmation of data preservation
   - Obtain certification of incident impact

2. **Contractual Obligations**
   - Review Data Processing Agreements (DPA)
   - Identify notification deadlines
   - Confirm processor responsibilities
   - Determine liability sharing

### Vendor Communication Template

- Incident overview and timeline
- Nature of data processed by vendor
- Specific systems/data affected
- Required actions and timeline
- Regulatory implications
- Confidentiality and communication requirements

## Supply Chain Assessment

### Incident Impact Analysis

1. **Identify Affected Vendors**
   - Which vendors had access to exposed data
   - Which vendors' systems were compromised
   - Which vendors have downstream customers

2. **Assess Downstream Impact**
   - Determine if vendor customers need notification
   - Evaluate joint liability
   - Plan coordinated disclosure

### Liability Management

- Review vendor insurance coverage
- Document vendor responsibility assessment
- Determine cost allocation
- Coordinate legal claims management

## Regulatory Coordination with Vendors

- Ensure vendor regulatory compliance
- Coordinate responses to regulator inquiries
- Manage public statement consistency
- Track vendor notification timeline

## Vendor Risk Assessment Post-Incident

- Evaluate vendor security posture
- Determine continued trust relationship
- Plan security improvements
- Document lessons learned
