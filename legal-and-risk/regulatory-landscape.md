#### NIS2 Directive
- **Notification Deadline**: 24 hours (alert), 72 hours (report), 30 days (final)
- **Scope**: Essential and important entities in critical sectors
- **Key Obligation**: Report significant incidents to national authority

#### DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act)
- **Notification Deadline**: 15 minutes to 1 hour for time-critical impact
- **Scope**: Financial entities and critical infrastructure providers
- **Key Obligation**: Report ICT incidents immediately

### United States

#### SEC Requirements
- **Notification Deadline**: Form 8-K within 4 business days
- **Scope**: Publicly traded companies with material impact
- **Key Obligation**: Disclose material cybersecurity incidents

#### State Data Breach Notification Laws
- **Notification Deadline**: 30-60 days (varies by state)
- **Scope**: All organizations handling resident data
- **Key Variations**: California (30 days), New York (expeditiously)

### Other Jurisdictions

- **PIPEDA** (Canada): ASAP to Privacy Commissioner
- **Privacy Act** (Australia): ASAP for serious harm
- **HIPAA** (US Healthcare): 60 days from discovery
- **PCI-DSS** (Payment Cards): 30 days to processors
