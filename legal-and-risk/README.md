## Overview

This section addresses the critical legal, regulatory, and risk management dimensions of cyber crisis response. It bridges the gap between technical incident response and organizational governance, ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations while managing business, reputational, and operational risks.

## Purpose

The Legal & Risk Management folder provides:

- **Regulatory compliance frameworks** for major cyber incident notification requirements (RGPD, NIS2, DORA, GDPR, SEC, etc.)
- **Legal notification procedures** with timelines and escalation protocols
- **Risk assessment methodologies** to evaluate incident severity and impact
- **Evidence preservation guidelines** for forensic investigations and legal proceedings
- **Third-party and supply chain considerations** during crisis response
- **Communication protocols** with legal stakeholders and regulatory bodies
- **Insurance and financial risk management** strategies
