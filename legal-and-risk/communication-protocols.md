# Communication Protocols

## Overview

This document establishes protocols for internal and external communication with legal, compliance, and regulatory stakeholders during cybersecurity incidents.

## Internal Communication

### Legal Team Notification

**Immediate Actions**:
- Notify General Counsel within 1 hour of incident discovery
- Brief Legal on initial assessment and timeline
- Discuss regulatory notification requirements
- Establish attorney-client privileged communication channel

**Ongoing Communication**:
- Daily legal briefings during active incident
- Weekly status updates post-incident
- Maintain legal hold procedures
- Document all legal recommendations

### Compliance & Risk Team

**Notification Timeline**:
- Chief Risk Officer: Within 2 hours
- Compliance Officer: Within 2 hours
- Data Protection Officer: Immediately

**Information to Share**:
- Incident summary and timeline
- Affected data types and volume
- Preliminary risk assessment
- Regulatory obligations identified
- Recommended actions

## External Communication - Regulatory Bodies

### Communication Standards

- All communications with regulators through Legal/Compliance
- Maintain records of all regulatory communications
- Document all regulatory requests
- Respond within specified deadlines
- Never admit liability or wrongdoing
- Coordinate across all applicable regulators

### Regulatory Engagement Strategy

**Proactive Approach**:
- Early engagement with primary regulator
- Provide preliminary notifications
- Demonstrate good faith and cooperation
- Regular status updates

**Reactive Approach**:
- Respond promptly to regulatory inquiries
- Provide requested information within deadlines
- Maintain professional, factual tone
- Escalate complex requests to Legal

## Media & Public Communications

### PR Strategy

- Coordinate with Legal before any public statements
- Maintain message consistency across channels
- Be transparent but factual
- Avoid speculation or admissions
- Focus on response and remediation

### Communication Channels

- Press releases (for material incidents)
- Social media statements
- Website updates
- Customer communication
- Media inquiries (Legal approval required)

## Crisis Communication Team

### Team Members

- Chief Executive Officer
- Chief Legal Officer
- Chief Information Security Officer
- Chief Communications Officer
- Data Protection Officer

### Meeting Schedule

- Daily during initial 72 hours
- Daily until stabilization
- Weekly updates thereafter
