# Insurance & Cyber Risk Management

## Overview

This document provides guidance on managing cyber insurance policies during incidents and optimizing insurance coverage for risk mitigation.

## Insurance Policy Review

### Pre-Incident Preparation

1. **Maintain Updated Policy Registry**
   - Cyber liability insurance carrier
   - Policy limits and deductibles
   - Coverage triggers and conditions
   - Insured vs. insurer responsibilities
   - Policy exclusions and limitations
   - Claims notification procedures

2. **Know Your Coverage**
   - Data breach response costs
   - Regulatory fines and penalties
   - Business interruption losses
   - Network security liability
   - Privacy liability
   - Extortion/ransomware coverage

## Incident Notification

### When to Notify Insurer

- Immediately upon incident discovery
- When potential claim appears likely
- When incident exceeds defined thresholds
- When regulatory investigation likely

### Information to Provide

- Date and time of incident discovery
- Nature of the incident
- Systems/data affected
- Preliminary damage estimate
- Potential claim amount
- Regulatory implications

## Claims Management

### Documentation Requirements

- All incident investigation reports
- Forensic investigation findings
- Regulatory correspondence
- Customer notification records
- Financial impact calculations
- Third-party vendor involvement

### Claim Process

1. Submit notification within policy deadline
2. Provide detailed incident documentation
3. Cooperate with insurer investigation
4. Track all incident-related costs
5. Submit claim within policy timeframe
6. Negotiate settlement if needed

## Cost Tracking

### Itemize All Expenses

- Forensic investigation and analysis
- Legal fees and consulting
- Regulatory notifications and responses
- Customer notification costs
- Credit monitoring services
- Public relations and communications
- IT remediation and recovery
- Business interruption losses

## Risk Mitigation

### Policy Improvements

Post-incident, assess:
- Coverage gaps identified
- Increased insurance limits needed
- Additional coverage types
- Vendor liability requirements
- Claims handling improvements

### Premium Optimization

- Document security improvements
- Demonstrate incident prevention efforts
- Track risk management improvements
- Negotiate better premiums
